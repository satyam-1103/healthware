Diamond Push Ups

A good illustration of this is the diamond push-up. While all types of push-ups strengthen the chest, shoulders, and triceps, putting your hands closer together than in a traditional push-up concentrates the attention on your triceps. Spread your hands wide apart to attack the chest, on the other hand.


How to do Diamond Push Ups


  1. 1. Place your mat on the floor. This exercise is best done on a mat, which will cushion your hands slightly. It may be difficult for your hands and wrists to do it on a hard surface.
  2. 2. Put yourself in a push-up position. To prepare, turn your back to the mat and stretch out your arms and legs a little.
  3. 3. Make a diamond formation with your hands under your chest. Make a fist with your forefingers and a fist with your thumbs. The diamond, also known as a pyramid, is formed as a result of this.
  4. 4. After getting down to the floor, push it up. Keep your back straight and your stomach and chest tense. Make sure your finger is in the correct diamond position. repeat.

Triceps Dips

The triceps dip is a great weight exercise that builds arm and shoulder strength. This simple exercise can be done almost anywhere and there are many variations to suit your fitness level. Use it as part of your upper body weight training session.

How to Do Triceps Dips

  1. 1. Push your palm to lift your body and slide it forward enough so that your butt sticks out from the edge of the chair.
  2. 2. Lower your body until your elbows bend between 45 and 90 degrees. Controls movement over the entire range of movement.
  3. 3. Gently push up your arm until it is almost straight and repeat the process.

Triceps Rope Push Downs

Triceps pushdown is one of the best exercises to develop your triceps. Versatile upper body workouts are usually done on a corded machine (a staple of most gyms), but you can use a resistance band at home or on the go to do some version of the exercise. Please Note this exercise needs an equipment : Pushdown machine.

Triceps Pushdown Workout GIFfrom

How to do Triceps Rope Pushdowns

  1. 1. Attach the rope handle to the tall pulley of the cable station. Bend your arms, separate your hands by shoulder width, and grab the bar with the overhand grips. Push your upper arm to both sides.
  2. 2. Without moving your upper arm, push down on the bar until your elbows close. It slowly returns to the starting position.
